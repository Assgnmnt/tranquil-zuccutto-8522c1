Do I need to complete the Discovery Lab first? +
Yes. The Activation Program is the second stage of The Assignment Room system. It is designed for women who have already defined their assignment and broken their primary internal agreement. Discovery Lab completion is required to enroll.

That depends on your assignment — but every woman leaves with something real and visible. Past graduates have launched coaching programs, ministry platforms, online courses, consulting offers, community groups, and creative bodies of work. The goal is a tangible launch, not a plan.
How much time does this require each week? +
Live sessions run 90 minutes per week. Building and tool work typically requires 3 to 4 additional hours. Total commitment is approximately 5 hours per week. This is a building program — it requires consistent work, not just attendance.
What happens after the Activation Program? +
Women who complete the Activation Program with a launched assignment are invited to apply for the Fellowship — the leadership stage of The Assignment Room where you learn to multiply your impact and help other women birth their assignments.
