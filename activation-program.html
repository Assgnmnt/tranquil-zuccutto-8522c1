A word of clarity

This is not for everyone.

The Activation Program™ is not for the woman who has not yet named her assignment. If you are still in the diagnosis or definition stage, begin with the private sessions or Discovery Lab™ first. This program requires a named assignment, existing clarity, and a commitment to build — not just attend.

Common questions

Before you apply.

How is this different from Discovery Lab™? +
Discovery Lab™ is the 4-week group room that activates the assignment — naming the agreement, defining the mandate, and producing your Assignment Brief™. The Activation Program™ is the 8-week sustainment container that follows. Discovery Lab™ activates. The Activation Program™ sustains.
Do I need Discovery Lab™ first? +
Discovery Lab™ or equivalent assignment clarity is strongly recommended. This program is designed for women who already have a named assignment and are ready to build execution rhythm around it. Applications are reviewed individually to confirm fit.
What will I have by week eight? +
A Rule of Life for Assignment™, an Advanced Activation Map™, a Curriculum Map™, Transformation Scorecard™ tracking, and a documented Sustainment Plan with your next 30–60 day proof milestone. The goal is a rhythm you can carry forward — not just a completed program.
What happens after the Activation Program™? +
Graduates with a launched assignment and demonstrated execution capacity are eligible to apply for the Fellowship™ — the facilitator formation pathway for women ready to multiply the framework and lead rooms for others.
