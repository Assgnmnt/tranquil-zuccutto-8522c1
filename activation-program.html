Do I need to complete the Discovery Lab first? +
Yes. The Charter Cohort is the second stage of The Assignment Room system. It is designed for women who have already completed The Reveal™ or Assignment Brief™ Intensive and are ready for structured group execution. Discovery Lab completion is required to enroll.
What will I have when the four weeks are over? +
You will have a terminated Breach of Agreement™, a finalized Mandate Statement, and your Assignment Brief™ PDF — delivered within 48 hours of your final session. These are not worksheets. They are working documents that travel with you into your next season.
What is the charter rate and why does it exist? +
The charter rate of $497 is available exclusively for the June 2026 pilot cohort — 5 women only. The regular price of the Activation Program is $997. Charter participants also receive a $497 credit toward the full Activation Program if they commit within 30 days of completing the cohort.
How much time does this require each week? +
Live sessions run 60–90 minutes per week. Pre-work and reflection between sessions typically requires 2–3 additional hours. This is a building room — it requires consistent presence, not just attendance.
What happens after the Charter Cohort? +
Charter Cohort graduates are invited to apply for the full Activation Program — the next stage of The Assignment Room where you build, execute, and launch what God showed you with full structure and accountability over an extended engagement.
