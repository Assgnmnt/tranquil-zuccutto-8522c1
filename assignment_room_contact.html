Before You Reach Out

These questions answer themselves - before a message is needed.

I don't know which offer is right for me. Where do I start?
Start with the free Readiness Diagnostic™. It takes about three minutes and identifies your current stage, your primary barrier, and the right next room. You will leave with a clear recommendation - no conversation required.
I took the diagnostic but I'm not sure about my result.
Book The Reveal™ ($197). That session is specifically designed to interpret your diagnostic result and help you understand what it is actually showing - so you leave with clarity about the next move, not just a stage name.
Is there a way to speak with Jackie before I book?
The best first step is the diagnostic - your result will tell you whether the right next room is a conversation like The Reveal™ or a direct booking like the Intensive. If you still have questions after that, reach out via the form above.
I want to book but I have a question about the policy first.
Review the full Refund & Cancellation Policy page. If your question is not answered there, include it in the form above and select the offer you are considering.
I'm interested in having Jackie speak or appear in media.
Use the contact form above and select "Media or speaking inquiry." Include the event name or outlet, the format, the date, and a brief description of the audience. Jackie's team will respond within 5–7 business days.
I'm a woman of faith but I'm not sure this space is for me.
The Assignment Room™ is faith-rooted and built for the woman who believes her assignment is God-given - and who is ready to stop circling it privately and build toward it with structure and intention. The About Jackie page has more on the faith foundation of the work.
