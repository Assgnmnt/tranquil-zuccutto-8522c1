Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: January 1, 2026

The Assignment Room™ is committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our digital content.

Our Commitment

We believe that access to information and services should not be limited by ability. The Assignment Room™ strives to meet or exceed the accessibility standards established by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA as defined by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

These guidelines help ensure that web content is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for users with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

Measures We Have Taken

In building and maintaining this website, we have taken the following measures to support accessibility:

  • Using semantic HTML to ensure proper structure and meaning for assistive technologies
  • Providing sufficient color contrast between text and background throughout the site
  • Ensuring that all interactive elements are keyboard-navigable
  • Including descriptive labels for form inputs and interactive controls
  • Designing for readability with legible font sizes, appropriate line spacing, and clear typographic hierarchy
  • Avoiding the use of content that flashes more than three times per second
  • Ensuring that text can be resized without loss of content or functionality

Known Limitations

While we work toward full accessibility, some areas of the website may not yet fully meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. Known limitations include:

  • Third-party embed content (such as booking systems, diagnostic tools, or video platforms) may not fully comply with accessibility standards. We encourage third-party vendors to improve their accessibility practices and will migrate to more accessible alternatives where possible.
  • Some older PDF documents or downloadable resources may not be fully optimized for screen readers. We are working to update these assets over time.

If you encounter a specific accessibility barrier not listed above, please let us know so we can address it.

Alternative Access

If you are unable to access any content or complete any process on this website due to a disability, please contact us and we will make reasonable efforts to provide the information or service you need in an alternative format. Options may include:

  • Email correspondence in place of web form submission
  • Phone or video session in place of written enrollment
  • Alternative document formats upon request

Feedback and Reporting

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you encounter accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@assignmentroom.com
  • Subject line: Accessibility Feedback

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and to address substantive issues within 30 days where feasible.

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time project. We are committed to regularly reviewing and improving the accessibility of this website as standards evolve and as we receive feedback from users. We will continue to assess new features, content, and third-party integrations against accessibility standards before and after launch.

External Resources

For more information on web accessibility, the following resources may be helpful:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) - W3C Web Accessibility Initiative
  • ADA.gov - Information on the Americans with Disabilities Act
