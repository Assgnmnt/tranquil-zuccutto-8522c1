What Happens in the Session

This is not a coaching call. It is a structured interpretation.

Every Reveal™ session follows the same intentional sequence - built to move you from a result on a page to a decision in your hands.

01
Live Diagnostic Interpretation
You stop trying to interpret your result alone.
Jackie walks through your diagnostic result with you - not just the stage name, but what it is revealing about where you are, what you have been protecting, and what the assignment is actually asking of you in this season.
You leave this part of the session understanding your result at a level a written report cannot give you.
02
Decision Audit™
You name the decision you have been circling.
The Decision Audit™ helps you identify the specific choice your assignment is requiring - not the vague sense that something needs to change, but the exact decision you have been talking around, rationalizing, and postponing.
Most women leave this step saying: "I already knew that. I just had not said it out loud."
03
Protection Check™
You see what the delay has been protecting.
The Protection Check™ helps you understand why staying stuck has felt safer than moving. Whether the delay has been protecting comfort, identity, recognition, security, or something else - you name it clearly rather than continuing to work around it.
This is the step that helps the delay stop feeling mysterious - and start feeling addressable.
04
Contradiction Move™
You leave with one immediate action that breaks the pattern.
The Contradiction Move™ is not a full life plan. It is one clear, specific action that proves the agreement no longer gets to govern your movement. It is designed to be taken within days - not someday.
Small enough to do this week. Significant enough to change the direction.
05
Pathway Recommendation
You know what to do next.
At the close of The Reveal™, Jackie recommends the next room based on what surfaced in the session - whether that is private definition through the Assignment Brief™ Intensive, communal movement through Discovery Lab™, or another path entirely.
You leave knowing the next right room - without pressure to commit to it immediately.
What You Leave With

Not more reflection. Something you can act on.

Every Reveal™ session produces four concrete outputs - regardless of how long the delay has been or how complex the assignment feels going in.

A Named Decision
The specific choice your assignment has been requiring - stated clearly, without the language you have been using to soften or avoid it.
The Protection You Named
An honest understanding of what the delay has been protecting - so it stops operating beneath the surface and starts being something you can address directly.
One Contradiction Move™
A single, specific action to take within days - not a full plan, but one clear move that breaks the pattern and proves the agreement no longer gets to lead.
A Recommended Next Room
One clear recommendation for what comes next in your process - the right room for where you are now, based on what surfaced in the session, not what the pricing ladder suggests.
Who This Is For

This session is for one specific moment.

The Reveal™ is designed for women at a particular stage - not every stage. If you are in the right place for it, you will know.

The Reveal™ is for the woman who has taken the diagnostic and received her result - and who knows that a result on a page is not the same as clarity she can act on. She needs the interpretation. She needs someone to help her see what the result is actually showing her about the decision underneath the delay.

It is also for the woman who has not yet taken the diagnostic but senses - clearly - that something is pulling on her and that she needs a focused, private room to name it with structure rather than circling it alone any longer.

This is not the right room if -

You have already named the decision and you are ready to document it. In that case, the Assignment Brief™ Intensive is the better fit. The Reveal™ is for the step before documentation - the step of interpretation and naming.

  • You took the diagnostic and have your result - but you are not sure what to do with it yet.
  • You have been in awareness or delay for long enough that the cost is starting to feel real.
  • You have tried to interpret the assignment privately - journaling, praying, processing - and you keep arriving at the same place without movement.
  • You want a structured conversation, not an open-ended coaching session. You want someone to help you see clearly, not just feel heard.
  • You are ready to name the decision - even if saying it out loud feels significant. You are done circling it alone.
