Side by Side

Intensive or Lab - which room is right for you?

Both offers help you move. The difference is format and setting. Here is how to choose.

Private & Direct
Assignment Brief™ Intensive
Investment: $797 · 2–3 hr private session
  • A 1:1 deep-dive with Jackie - no group setting
  • You define the assignment and identify the internal agreement delaying movement
  • You leave with your Assignment Brief™ PDF and Activation Map™ in hand
  • Accelerated, focused, and built for women who want direct clarity without a group process
  • Best if you want private, intensive work with a documented output at the end
Book the Intensive
Communal & Guided
Discovery Lab™
Investment: $997 · 4-week small-group room (5 women max)
  • A small-group room - intimate, accountable, and witnessed
  • Four weekly sessions, each with a specific outcome and a cumulative arc
  • Guided teaching, structured reflection, and peer accountability throughout
  • You also leave with your Assignment Brief™ and Activation Map™
  • Best if you want teaching, rhythm, community, and guided movement over time
Join Discovery Lab™
