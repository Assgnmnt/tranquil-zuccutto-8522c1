The Assignment Brief™ Intensive ($497 · 3 hours) goes deeper — your agreement terminated, your mandate defined, your plan built, and your Assignment Brief™ delivered within 48 hours. It is for women who want a private fast-track from insight to documented action.
How are these different from the Discovery Lab™ Charter Cohort? +
The Reveal™ and Assignment Brief™ Intensive are private — 1:1 with Jackie Parker. Discovery Lab™ is a 4-week small-group experience with teaching, witness, rhythm, and accountability. If you want a private conversation, start here. If you want a group container, go to Discovery Lab™.
Do I need to know my assignment before I book? +
No. These sessions are specifically designed for women who feel called but cannot yet fully define or articulate their assignment. Take the free Assignment Readiness Diagnostic™ first to confirm your stage — it takes 3 minutes and will tell you exactly where you are.
Which session is right for me? +
If you need clarity on what is holding you back — start with The Reveal™. If you are ready to terminate the agreement, define the mandate, and leave with a documented plan — book the Assignment Brief™ Intensive. If you want a group experience over four weeks — go to Discovery Lab™.

Select your session above — The Reveal™ or Assignment Brief™ Intensive. Morgan Ellis, our Client Experience Coordinator, will follow up within 24 hours to confirm your session date and send your pre-work form. Complete the pre-work before your session so Jackie can review it in advance.
