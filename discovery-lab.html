Common questions

Before you apply.

How is Discovery Lab™ different from the private sessions? +
The Reveal™ and Assignment Brief™ Intensive are private 1:1 sessions with Jackie. Discovery Lab™ is a small-group room with teaching, witness, and peer accountability over four weeks. If you need private diagnosis or definition first, start with the private sessions. If you are ready for group movement, apply for Discovery Lab™.
Do I need to complete anything before applying? +
Yes. The Assignment Readiness Diagnostic™ is required before applying. It is free and takes 3 minutes. It identifies your current stage and ensures Discovery Lab™ is the right next room for where you are. Take it at assignmentroom.com/diagnostic.
What will I have when the four weeks are over? +
A terminated Breach of Agreement™, a finalized Mandate Statement, an Activation Map™, a Proof of Movement™ document, a Decision Lock™, and your Assignment Brief™ PDF. These are not reflections. They are working documents that travel with you into your next season.
What is the investment? +
The standard investment for Discovery Lab™ is $997 for the full 4-week experience. Cohort size is limited. Applications are reviewed before enrollment is confirmed.
What happens after Discovery Lab™? +
Discovery Lab™ participants who complete the program with a documented Assignment Brief™ and defined mandate are eligible to apply for the Activation Program™ — the 8-week sustainment and execution container where you build rhythm, capacity, and measurable momentum around the assignment.
