Where The Fellowship fits.

The Fellowship™ is the final room — for women who have moved through the full Assignment Room™ system and are now formed to facilitate rooms and raise others.

The Diagnostic reveals. The Forensic Report™ diagnoses. The Reveal™ turns diagnosis into decision. The Intensive defines. Discovery Lab™ activates. Activation Program™ sustains. Fellowship™ multiplies.

What you build inside the Fellowship

Three pillars.
Twelve weeks. Facilitator formation + certification review.

The Fellowship is built around three pillars that move you from a woman on assignment to a woman who builds Assignment Rooms for others.

Spiritual Leadership Identity

You carry authority now. These weeks establish your posture as a facilitator — not a peer — and develop your ability to guide other women through the assignment journey with conviction, discernment, and skill.

Facilitation + Guided Discernment

You learn to use approved Assignment Room™ diagnostic frameworks as a facilitator — guiding other women through awakening, discernment, breakthrough, activation, and sustainment using the system's proprietary tools.

Room Design + Multiplication

You design and prepare to launch your own Assignment Room — a small group, a cohort, or a community — using the frameworks built inside this system. Facilitator rights are granted through the certification review process and written agreement.

This is not for everyone.

The Fellowship is not for the woman who wants access to the tools without submitting to the formation process. If you are looking for a shortcut to the framework, this is not that.
