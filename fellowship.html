What you build inside the Fellowship

Three pillars.
Twelve weeks. One certification.

The Fellowship is built around three pillars that move you from a woman on assignment to a woman who builds Assignment Rooms for others.

Leadership Identity

You carry authority now. These weeks establish your posture as a leader — not a peer — and develop your ability to guide other women through the assignment journey with conviction and skill.

Teaching and Coaching

You learn to use all five Assignment Room diagnostic tools as a facilitator — guiding other women through awakening, discernment, breakthrough, activation, and sustainment.

Building Rooms

You design and launch your own Assignment Room — a small group, a cohort, a community, or a program — using the systems Jackie built. You leave certified and equipped to lead.
