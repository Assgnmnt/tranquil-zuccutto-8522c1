The Story

I spent 30 years at the top of the ladder. And almost didn't make it off.

As Senior Vice President at Global Payments — a $7.4B NYSE Fortune 500 company operating across 40 countries — I led thousands, shaped succession governance, and sat in rooms most people only read about.

And I almost died with my assignment inside me. Not because God hadn't spoken. Because I kept negotiating with what He showed me.

"Most women don't reject their assignment. They negotiate with it."

When my position was eliminated in January 2025, I stopped negotiating. I launched The Assignment Room™ — because I refuse to die with this inside me. And I refuse to let you die with yours.
