Meet your guide

Jackie Parker —
Founder, The Assignment Room™

Jackie Parker spent decades at the top of the ladder — Senior Vice President at Global Payments, a $7.4B NYSE-listed Fortune 500 fintech operating across 40+ countries and 27,000 employees. She served as Chief Talent and Diversity Officer, and as President of the General Motors Foundation. She led teams of thousands, shaped succession governance for Top 22 executives, and sat in rooms that most people only read about.

And then God called her off the ladder.

The Assignment Room™ was not born from theory. It was born from lived experience — navigating the gap between what God showed her and what fear, security, and identity were telling her to protect. Jackie holds an MS from Johns Hopkins, a BS from Hampton University, and is a Certified Professional Coach. She built this system because she refused to die with her assignment inside her — and she refuses to let you die with yours.
