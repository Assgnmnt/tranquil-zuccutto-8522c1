Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025 · assignmentroom.com

The Assignment Room™ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit assignmentroom.com or purchase our products and programs.

1. Who we are

The Assignment Room™ is owned and operated by Jackie Parker, based in Atlanta, Georgia. We provide transformation programs, diagnostic tools, and digital products designed to help women identify and execute their God-given assignments.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@assignmentroom.com

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address when you take the Readiness Diagnostic™
  • Name, email, and program information when you apply for or enroll in a program
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe (we never store your card details)
  • Responses to diagnostic assessments and application forms
  • Communications you send to us via email

We also collect non-personal information automatically, including browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site. This helps us improve your experience.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver the programs, products, and services you have purchased or enrolled in
  • Send you your diagnostic results and personalized next steps
  • Communicate with you about your enrollment, cohort dates, and program updates
  • Send you emails about The Assignment Room™ — you may opt out at any time
  • Improve our website, programs, and diagnostic tools
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How we protect your information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption on all pages of our website. Payment information is processed exclusively through Stripe, which maintains PCI DSS compliance. We never store your credit card or banking details on our servers.

Access to your personal information is restricted to Jackie Parker and any team members who need it to deliver your program or respond to your inquiries.

5. Third-party services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Their privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy
  • Formspree — Form submissions and contact forms
  • Netlify — Website hosting
  • Google Workspace — Email communication

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Digital products and refund policy

Due to the digital and downloadable nature of our products — including the Assignment Readiness Forensic Report™ — all sales are final once the product has been delivered.

If you experience a technical issue accessing your purchased product, please contact us at support@assignmentroom.com within 48 hours of purchase and we will resolve it promptly.

For program enrollments (Discovery Lab, Activation Program, Fellowship), refund terms are communicated at the time of enrollment confirmation.

7. Email communications

When you take our Readiness Diagnostic™ or submit a form on our website, you may receive emails from us including your results, program information, and updates from Jackie Parker. You may unsubscribe from these emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email or by contacting us directly at support@assignmentroom.com.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to function properly. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of certain features on our site.

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@assignmentroom.com and we will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's privacy

Our programs and products are designed for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately at support@assignmentroom.com.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website or programs after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
