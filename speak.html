Flagship Keynote

Stop Negotiating. Move.

The internal agreements keeping high-achievers from their next assignment — and how to break them.

Most high-achievers are not confused about what they are called to do. They are protecting something — security, identity, recognition — that feels too expensive to release. This keynote exposes the specific internal agreements that masquerade as wisdom, patience, and responsibility. It names what most audiences have never heard named. And it forces a decision.

Personal Story Keynote

From the Boardroom to the Assignment

A personal keynote on reinvention, obedience, and building what God showed you.

Jackie spent more than two decades at the top of the ladder — SVP at Global Payments, Chief Talent Officer, President of the GM Foundation. And then God called her off it. This is the story of that moment, the fear that followed, and the decision that produced The Assignment Room™. It is the talk that answers: what does it actually look like to step off the ladder you spent years climbing?

The Assignment Economy™

Why clarity without execution is costing more than you think.

Clarity has an expiration date. This talk addresses the most expensive mistake high-achieving professionals make — accumulating insight, strategy, and vision without converting any of it into execution. Using the Assignment Economy™ framework, Jackie shows audiences how to move from perpetual preparation to measurable, sustained action. Built for corporate, entrepreneurial, and marketplace ministry audiences who are tired of knowing without building.
