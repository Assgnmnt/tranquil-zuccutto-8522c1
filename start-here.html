Common questions

Everything you need
to know before booking.

Is The Reveal™ the same as the Forensic Report™? +
No. The Forensic Report™ is the written diagnosis — it shows your score matrix, negotiation profile, dominant agreement, protected rung, Assignment Gap™, and prescribed pathway. The Reveal™ is the private decision session. It helps you identify what the diagnosis is asking you to decide. They are different products with different purposes.
Do I need the Forensic Report™ before booking The Reveal™? +
No, but it is recommended. You can book The Reveal™ after completing the free Readiness Diagnostic™. If you have already ordered your Forensic Report™, Jackie will use it as part of the session. Either way, the session begins where your data ends.
What do I leave with from The Reveal™? +
You leave with a named decision, a clearer understanding of what delay has been protecting, one Contradiction Move™ — a single action that interrupts the pattern — and a recommended next pathway. You will not leave with a document. You will leave with a decision.
What is the difference between The Reveal™ and the Assignment Brief™ Intensive? +
The Reveal™ helps you name the decision your diagnostic exposed. The Assignment Brief™ Intensive helps you define the mandate, terminate the agreement, build the first move, and leave with your Assignment Brief™ PDF. The Reveal™ is diagnosis-to-decision. The Intensive is decision-to-documentation.
Do I need to know my assignment before I book? +
No. These sessions are specifically designed for women who feel called but cannot yet fully define or articulate their assignment. Take the free Assignment Readiness Diagnostic™ first — it takes 3 minutes and will tell you exactly where you are.
How do I book my session? +
Select your session above — The Reveal™ or Assignment Brief™ Intensive. Morgan Ellis, our Client Experience Coordinator, will follow up within 24 hours to confirm your session date and send your pre-work form. Complete the pre-work before your session so Jackie can review it in advance.
